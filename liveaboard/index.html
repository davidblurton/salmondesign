The Liveaboard Trust
Identity

The Liveaboard Trust is a soon-to-be charity organisation in Cambridge which supports those who live aboard longboats in and around the county.

I was asked to create them an identity which promoted community spirit and social inclusion.

I focused on themes of support and connectivity, incorporating nautical motifs with a bold, professional feel.
